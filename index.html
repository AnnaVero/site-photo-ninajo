About me

I'm ballet photographer based in Tokyo Japan and am part of the team at Photobank.com. I've been shooting ballet for over 10 years and have many years of experience in Japan with the Tokyo Ballet Company and Tokyo Metropolitan Ballet.

In the past I have also worked with many other ballet companies in Japan, such as the Kyoto Ballet, the Kanto Theatre Ballet and the Nara Ballet. I also have a background in photography and a degree in Photography, so I understand the needs of a ballet company and how to shoot ballet.
